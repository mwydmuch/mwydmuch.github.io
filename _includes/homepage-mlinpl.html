ML in PL

I'm proud to be a member of the awesome ML in PL Association, a non-profit organization devoted to fostering the machine learning community in Poland and around the world.

I co-organized many events in years 2019-2023, such as ML in PL Conference or MLSS^S serving as Call for Contributions (Talks and Posters), Website, and IT Infrastructure coordinator. I was a member of the management board of the association in 2021 and 2022.
